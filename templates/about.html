My name is Omar Chaarawi and I am an aspiring software developer and data scientist/engineer. I live in Boulder, Colorado where I am working as an arborist while I pursue a computer science education at CU Boulder. I first became interested in comp sci during an internship at MIT while I was earning my bachelor degree in chemistry at Ithaca College. I had the opportunity to work in a computational chemistry lab as an undergraduate research assistant. I was building models of molecules and using some physical chemistry computations way over my head. It was some really cool stuff, and I wish I had just switched over to computer science then. I ended up finishing my chemistry degree and did a little wandering while trying to determine what I really wanted to do. I found a free intro to computer science course through Harvard and was absolutely hooked after completing that. I enrolled in classes at CU Boulder and am planning on going through their Data Science masters program in the fall of 2021.

While taking Harvard's CS50: Intro to Computer Science class, there was a project developing a small webpage for conducting mock trades. The site made use of API's used to pull stock data in real time and databases were used to maintain a record of user data. This was my initial exposure to building a website from scratch using a Flask framework. Omarseven.com is my personal website that I have built in an effort to represent some of my projects as well as further develop my website development skills. I have some other programming projects in various languages listed under the projects tab.

Programming obviously requires a lot of my time, but I am still able to also do a lot of things that I love and help me maintain balance while pursueing such a challenging field. I am big into running, climbing, backcountry skiing, mountain biking, and some other things the great state of Colorado is known for. If you're from Colorado and have a strava add me. I am always looking for new places to run..
